Privacy Policy

Welcome to Learner! Kids Learning Games. Your privacy and your child’s safety are very important to us.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our application.

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect limited information to improve the learning experience.

  • App usage statistics
  • Learning progress
  • Device information
  • Crash reports and diagnostics

We do NOT collect sensitive personal information from children.

2. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for children aged 6–9. We are committed to protecting children's privacy and complying with child privacy regulations.

We do not knowingly collect:

  • Full names
  • Phone numbers
  • Email addresses from children
  • Precise location data
  • Photos or videos

3. How We Use Information

Information collected may be used to:

  • Improve educational content
  • Track learning progress
  • Fix bugs and crashes
  • Improve app performance
  • Enhance user experience

4. Offline Learning

Most app features work offline. Your learning data may remain stored locally on your device.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services such as:

  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Google Play Services

These services may collect anonymous technical information to improve app stability and performance.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data and maintain app security.

7. Ads & Purchases

Our goal is to provide a safe and child-friendly experience.

Any advertisements or in-app purchases, if included, will be designed responsibly and appropriately for children.

8. Parent Controls

Parents may have access to learning progress and educational reports in future updates.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
